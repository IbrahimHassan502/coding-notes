cover letter

general tips

  • for your first few clients show them what they will get for working with a freelancer who doesn't have any reviews, privileges like:
    • I can start working on your project right away
    • being my only client helps me to work closely with you
    • to be responsive and focused on communicating with you, and sending you updates regularly
    • answering all your questions and concerns
    • I'll be motivated to work with you as long as it takes to get the results you want with the best quality you can ask for and even exceed those
    • I truly care about your project and I will help whenever I see a chance even if it's not in the task you reqiure from me
    • having only your project on my plate makes it easy for to rely on me to complete the project within the specified timeframe
