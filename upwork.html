profile title

according to upwork it should be informative and tells the clients what you do

profile description

according to upwork keep in mind that only the first 250 characters will appear in the freelancer search results list.

you could outline your relevant skills and how hiring you would add value to a potential client's business. Consider including within the rest of your profile overview tools relevant to your craft, highlighting experience that coincides with the projects you'd like to work on and experience with specific industries.

Try to avoid more generic words and instead use your profile overview to highlight keywords relevant to the projects you are interested in. Try reading through projects you'd like to bid on and pulling your relevant experience, skills, or tools from those projects for your profile overview.

Important: never include your personal contact information in your profile; this is against the Upwork Terms of Service.

calculate hourly rate

few questions you need to ask yourself

How much do I need to earn to support my lifestyle? What is the ideal amount I want to earn a month? What do other freelancers charge for similar services? What expenses do I have as a freelance business owner? Are my skills in demand?

calculate hourly rate in numbers

For example, $50,000(USD) per year breaks down like this: $50,000 per year (before taxes) Working 40 hours per week (5 days per week x 8 hour days) 4 weeks off for vacations, sick days, and unexpected absences = 48 working weeks x 40 hours per week = 1920 working hours = $50,000 / 1920 hours = $26 per working hour
